Features

Proposal.to is an online petition platform
that's hosted by the people, for the people.


Unbiased

The entire platform is run by the people, for the people-- on the Ethereum blockchain. There is no central organization running this website, and no views or perspectives being artificially promoted.


Censorship-resistant

Since Proposal.to is decentralized and this website doesn't host any content, we have no control over the topics allowed.


Lots To Come

We have a full roadmap of features we're building out, including advanced searching abilities, login with Blockstack, and subsidized gas fees.
